1:1 Mentorship for Filmmakers & Actors

Tailored Support for Every Stage of Your Career

Whether you're just starting out or already building momentum, this mentorship is designed to meet you where you are. I offer personal, practical, one-on-one coaching for writers, directors, and performers navigating the real world of film and television, from indie to studio.

  • Story and script development – from creation to sale
  • Standing out and getting seen
  • Self-tapes and audition tips and tricks
  • Pitching and pitch deck refinement
  • Film festivals
  • Moving from short to feature
  • Getting an agent

These sessions are grounded in industry experience, with a focus on the questions that come up again and again:

  • Should I go to film school?
  • How do I get an agent; or better yet, how do I make agents want me?
  • What's the best way to meet producers and build meaningful connections?
  • What makes a pitch or script compelling enough to stand out?
  • Should I be writing a short or a feature right now?
  • How do I move from indie to studio; or from short films to features?
  • What should I expect from film festivals, and how do I get in?
  • How do I get seen as a self-directing actor or filmmaker?

If you're asking yourself any of these questions, this mentorship is designed for you. Every session is customised to your goals and current challenges, helping you take clear, confident steps forward.
