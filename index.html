Directed by Oliver Park and released in 2022 (Lionsgate, Decal).

Currently streaming on Netflix, The Offering opened in over 400 theaters across the United States and was quickly recognized as one of the standout independent horror films of the year. The film ranked in the top 10 horror releases on several major VOD platforms, including Apple and Amazon, and received praise for its chilling atmosphere and bold direction. It became the #1 horror film on iTunes UK, and was selected as a Rotten Tomatoes “Certified Fresh”.
